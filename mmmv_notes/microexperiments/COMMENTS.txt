
Microexperiments are small, dirty, 

    NOT REUSABLE HACKS	

that are done for calculating something or done for trying something out.
Their main purpose is to allow one to form a bit more 
informed opinion about various things that one encounters or
to illustrate something in some debate.

The dirty hacks are reference points, data, for discussions 
with friends or with people that I either know or 
I have encountered somewhere, possibly online.
