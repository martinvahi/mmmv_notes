===========================================================================
========================  mmmv_symbols_t1  ================================
===========================================================================

The mmmv_symbols_t1 is a collection of symbol descriptions in
various formats. For example, individual letters of a font-file
are symbols. Electronic schematics use symbols. Flowcharts
use symbols. Clip-art vector drawings are symbols.
Vector graphics descriptions of company logos are symbols.
The letters of the Rosetta Stone

    https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rosetta_Stone
    
are symbols. Sound combinations of tone-dialing phones

    http://hyperphysics.phy-astr.gsu.edu/hbase/Sound/telton.html
    (archival copy: http://archive.fo/l8KWv )
    
    https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dual-tone_multi-frequency_signaling

are symbols. Hand gestures of sign languages

    https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_sign_languages
    
are symbols and those symbols can be stored in multiple formats, including
robotic hand control command sequences. Music instrument descriptions
of MIDI-file players are symbols. Phonemes

    https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phoneme
    
of speech synthesis systems are symbols. Morse-code alphabet and its
various analogues consists of symbols. Mathematical formulae consist
of symbols. Chess pieces are symbols.
